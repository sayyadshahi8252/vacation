A Sofa For The Whole Family

This nice looking sofa can easily turn your evening into an entertaining movie show. In just a couple of movements, it’s transformed into a lounging area for the entire family. Its large size make it suitable for a high number of people, but what makes it special (and family friendly) is its multifunctional characteristic. With 4 individual soft pieces which can be added to make the boomerang shape into a more comfortable square that can be enjoyed by kids as well as grown-ups. The design was specially oriented to making a night watching a movie a more casual experience, with people laying around, instead of standing. The sofa is also suited for sleeping, maybe rivaling a king-size bed with ease. You will have to try one before you make the decision. This sofa is available here..
